    Handles DHCP packets

    Functions

    decode/1

    Decodes a DHCP packet into a #dhcp_message{} record. Fields are deserialized into Erlang types where possible. Not all sub-options are exhaustively deserialized. In such cases, the binary is passed through to the consumer.

    encode/1

    Encodes a #dhcp_message{} record into a DHCP packet. Fields are serialized per RFC 2131, section 2: https://datatracker.ietf.org/doc/html/rfc2131#section-2
